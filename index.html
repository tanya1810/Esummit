About

E-SUMMIT 2021

E-summit is an annual gathering of entrepreneurs, investors, and their supporters in a three-day event organized by EDC, TIET. Here, we celebrate the spirit of entrepreneurship and provide a platform to all the young minds to present their ingenuity and imagination. The summit is a symposium of sharp and creative minds, path-leading professionals and innovators, who believe in the power of an idea and its ability to take the whole world by a storm. The event acts as a platform where today's youth can mingle to share their innovative and enterprising ideas which is the lifeblood of entrepreneurship.

Events

23 April'21

UNWIND

A never seen before event where fear is shed and anxiety drops to find more confidence along the path. With brewing curiosity EDC brings to you its informal entrepreneurial eve UNWIND, an enthraliing event packed with fun and frolic. The incredible perfomance of experts boost up the spirit to let the students unwind.

24 April'21

ELEVATE

A platform to experiment with how well people perform under pressure and a stage to exhibit technical proficiency and business aptitude. It is a B-Plan competition wherein participants will have to procure resources and optimize the cost in order to maintain the leaderboards. To be precise, a mock trading process that will help the participants to learn about the risk factors and how to manage the resources.

INTERNSHIP FAIR

EDC brings you the perfect opportunity to start the climb of your professional ladder. Internship fair, a platform which provides an insight about industry.
